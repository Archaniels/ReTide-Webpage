# Project Overview
## Background
ReTide is a Laravel-based website that utilizes Laravel/PHP as the foundation of the website, NodeJS to handle CRUD operations between the website and the database, Supabase as the remote database, and TailwindCSS as the main front-end framework.

ReTide serves the purpose to increase awareness and attention to the dangers of irresponsible garbage and waste throwing (in accordance to Sustainable Development Goals 14: Life Below Water), specifically on the impact it has on bodies of water (lakes, rivers, ocean, etc).

ReTide offers a number of services, some of which includes Marketplace, Blog, and Donation to achieve this goal. Marketplace offers a platform where users can purchase, and sell reused products that are recycled from collected waste found in water bodies, which in turn reduces the quantities of waste. Alternatively, Blog teaches and raises awareness of the dangers of waste by providing education, guides, recommendations, best practice, and many more. Finally, Donation is used as a way to encourage and help expand ReTide to help it cover, collect, and repurpose more waste.
