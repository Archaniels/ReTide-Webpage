# Product

## ReTide Website

## Register

product

## Users

People living near seawaters who have first-hand seen the impact and effect of irresponsible trash disposal or have limited access to proper regulated trash disposal. They want to buy eco-friendly products made from recycled ocean waste.

## Product Purpose

To provide a marketplace for eco-friendly products crafted from recycled ocean waste, turning local environmental challenges into a sustainable circular economy. Success looks like users confidently purchasing items while feeling they are contributing to ocean conservation.

## Brand Personality

Eye-catching, modern, different. Evokes eco-consciousness and radiates trust.

## Anti-references

- Amazon and eBay (cluttered, overly utilitarian, lacking brand soul).

## Design Principles

1. **Radiate Trust**: Every interaction, from viewing a product to checking out, must feel secure, professional, and reliable.
2. **Modern & Different**: Break away from generic, cluttered e-commerce templates. Use bold layouts, negative space, and a premium dark aesthetic.
3. **Eco-consciousness as a Core**: The design should subtlely remind users of the positive environmental impact of their purchase.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Ensure high contrast for the dark theme, especially with the teal accent (`#63CFC0`). Support standard keyboard navigation for all interactive elements (cart, checkout).
